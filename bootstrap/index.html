№ 01

We launched 3 years ago

We launched three years ago and loved it every second. At the time I was just a man with an ideea but 2 weeks later I was a man with a plan. We jumped on this adventure and learned a lot along the way. Two days after launching we had our first client.

№ 02

We believe creativity has no limits

In what we do, being creative is crucial. And we love the moments when we have to make our brain work and produce new, crazy, beautiful ideas. It's one of the best parts of our work.

№ 03

We like challenges

We love a good challenge. Photoshoots in the rain, cold, snowy, winter days, travel photoshoots - you name it, we are exited for all of them. And most of the times, the most unusual people, places and conditions provide the best results.
